Privacy & Policy for ZEN ZONES

At ZEN ZONES, we are committed to protecting the privacy of our users. This Privacy Policy outlines the types of personal information we collect, how it is used, and the measures we take to safeguard your data while using our website.

1. Information Collection

  • Personal Information: We may collect certain personally identifiable information when you use our website, such as your name, email address, and any other information voluntarily provided by you.
  • Non-Personal Information: We may also collect non-personal information, including device information, browser type, IP address, and anonymous usage data, to improve our website and enhance user experience.

2. Use of Information

  • Core Functionalities: The ZEN ZONES extension may use your personal information to provide core functionalities, such as a search box and clock, to ensure a smooth user experience.
  • Daily Wellness Tip and Guided Meditation: In order to deliver the daily wellness tip and guided meditation, we may use your information to customize content that aligns with your interests and preferences.
  • Reminder to Take Breaks: We may use your data to send you periodic reminders encouraging you to take breaks for better well-being while using our extension.
  • Peaceful Nature Scenes: Your non-personal information, such as device data and browsing behavior, may be used to tailor and display peaceful nature scenes as backgrounds for a more relaxing experience.

3. Data Security

  • We take data security seriously and implement reasonable measures to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, disclosure, or alteration.
  • However, please note that no online platform can guarantee 100% security, and you acknowledge that any information shared with us is done at your own risk.

4. Third-Party Services

  • ZEN ZONES may use third-party services, such as analytics tools or advertising networks. These services may collect and use information as per their own privacy policies.
  • We encourage you to review the privacy policies of any third-party services used by ZEN ZONES.

5. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

  • ZEN ZONES may use cookies and other tracking technologies to enhance your user experience and gather non-personal information.
  • You have the option to disable cookies through your browser settings, but this may impact certain functionalities of our website.

6. Children's Privacy

  • ZEN ZONES is not intended for use by children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

7. Changes to the Privacy Policy

  • We reserve the right to update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page, and the revised date will be mentioned at the top.

By using ZEN ZONES, you consent to the terms outlined in this Privacy Policy. If you have any questions or concerns about our privacy practices, please contact us.
